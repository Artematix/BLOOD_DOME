# Player Quick Reference

## The Basics

- You start at **Level 5** with a premade character
- Kill enemies and players to earn **coins**
- Spend coins at the **Shop** for upgrades
- Complete **quests** for bonus rewards
- Survive **events** and hazards
- **Last one standing wins**

## Your Turn

*Standard D&D 5e action economy applies unless noted.*

- Action
- Bonus Action
- Movement
- Reaction
- Free Object Interaction

## The Shop

Visit the shop to:
- Buy weapons, armor, items
- Buy new abilities
- Sell unwanted items (50% of buy price)

## Quests

- Check your character sheet for personal quests
- Universal quests available to everyone
- Rewards: coins, items, abilities
